The Kingdom of Altonia

In Short

  • Second most advanced human realm after the Free States
  • Known for technological achievements and aggressive foreign policy
  • Manufacturing sector facing challenges from increased competition from the Free Cities

The Land and People of Altonia

  • Diverse kingdom with a range of landscapes and climates
  • Predominantly human, with a small minority of halflings and other non-human races
  • Mostly rural population living in small towns and villages, with several large cities on the western coast
  • People are hardy and resilient, having adapted to challenges of their environment

The Economy of Altonia

  • Diverse and dynamic, with a focus on agriculture, mining, and trade
  • Northern regions known for livestock farming and dairy production
  • Central plains known for grain production and fruit and vegetable cultivation
  • Western coast known for fishing and trade
  • Southern borderlands known for forestry and hunting
  • Eastern mountains known for mining and smelting
  • Manufacturing sector struggling due to increased competition from the Free Cities, shifting focus towards exporting raw materials and resources to the Free States

The Politics of Altonia

  • Ruled by a monarchy (King Harold II)
  • Advised by council of nobles representing the interests of various regions and factions
  • Known for aggressive foreign policy, currently at war with Kingdom of Mire over border dispute
  • Both sides importing Free City weaponry during the conflict

The war with Mire

  • Silver mines on the border
  • Altonia is desperate for silver due to deflation caused by importing of Elven luxuries
  • Invaded mire to occupy the border lands over a flimsy old claim

The addiction to Elven Goods

  • Many nobles love Elven luxuries
  • Most addictive are Elven wines
  • Massive flow of silver and gold away from Altonia towards The Empire of the Sun
  • Many nobles taken to squeezing their peasants for currency to fuel their addictions
  • Unrest is rapidly increasing due to these abuses

The religions of the Kingdom of Altonia:

  • The Church of the Light: dominant religion based on worship of single deity called the Light; preaches importance of moral virtue and pursuit of goodness; strict moral code and hierarchical structure; opposes other religions and belief systems; strong following and deeply ingrained in kingdom's culture
  • The Way of the Elements: nature-based religion popular among indigenous peoples of southern borderlands, based on belief in spiritual energy in nature that can be harnessed through rituals and ceremonies
  • The Cult of the Dragon: secretive religion widely regarded as heretical, based on worship of dragons believed to possess great magical abilities; rumored to be involved in dark arts and blood magic
  • The Way of the Sword: warrior religion popular among knights and soldiers, based on belief in martial prowess as highest form of virtue and true strength coming from discipline, courage, and self-sacrifice; emphasizes importance of honor and service to kingdom
  • The Cult of the Dragon: secretive religion with rituals and hierarchy, rumored to be involved in dark arts and blood magic
  • The Temple of the Arcane: religion centered on the study and practice of magic, with a hierarchical structure and emphasis on knowledge and discipline; often viewed with suspicion by the Church of the Light
