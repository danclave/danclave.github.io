The Black Cat Tavern

Tags
Characters		“Silent" Joe, "Sly" Sally, Captain" Kenny, "Mad" Mike, Whisper" Will, Slick, "Big" Tony
Parent location		The Barnacles
Sub-location

Upon entering the Black Cat Tavern, the guest is greeted with a variety of sounds and smells. The sound of soft chatter and laughter fills the air, as patrons gather around tables and in plush armchairs. The crackling of the fire in the corner provides a cozy ambiance, and the clinking of glasses can be heard from the bar. The smell of fresh-baked bread, roasting meats, and simmering stews fills the air, making the tavern feel warm and inviting.

In the center of the tavern, there is a large pot with a "forever stew" bubbling away. It's considered good manners for patrons to bring something to add to the pot when entering a halfling home, and the stew is a communal meal that is always available to anyone who is hungry. The pot is tended by the halfling owners and is always simmering with a variety of meats, vegetables, and herbs, giving it a rich and hearty aroma.

The tavern's atmosphere is designed to feel like a halfling borough home, which is why the owners have added a big pot, it's a common practice in halfling culture to share meals and cook together. The players will notice that the tavern is a comfortable and welcoming place, where everyone is encouraged to relax and enjoy a warm meal and a cold drink.
